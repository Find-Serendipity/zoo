Wild Animal Mini-Zoo Animal Introduction Factsheet

Welcome Zookeeper Interns!

Bears
  1. Ollie

    2. Ollie loves to sit in the sun and wave to visitors, and loves to eat cabbages.

  2. Mona

    3. Mona likes to eat and will often beg you for snacks. Mind your picnic basket!

Monkeys
  1. Cookie

    2. If you give Cookie a banana, he will very soon want to rifle through all your pockets for snacks.

  2. Earl

    3. Earl is an old man who always gets to eat first, regardless of the fuss the younger monkeys make.

  3. Banana Pudding

    4. B.P. is a sweet girl who loves to cuddle! You may end up with a backpack known as Banana Pudding if you don't watch out.

Giraffes
  1. Frankie

    2. Frankie will break your heart with her big brown eyes and lick your face with her super wet tongue. You've been warned.

  2. Coconut

    3. Coconut is our baby giraffe! She currently has a special team of handlers, but we welcome you to go say hi.

Lions
  1. Mella

    2. Mella loves her ball, and woe to the person who takes it away from her! She loves salmon as a snack.

  2. Karl

    3. Karl is our resident King of the Rock. He loves to sun himself on the outcropping any time the sun is out. He is never late for meals, however.

Alligators
  1. Wren

    2. Wren is the largest of our alligators. Her favorite snack is whole chickens.

  2. Aspen

    3. Don't let Aspen fool you - she is fast! If you don't watch your ankles when you enter her enclosure, she may take your feet.

  3. Mika

    4. Mika is so chill that the birds land on her all the time. She enjoys steak on her birthday.
